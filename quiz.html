TECH-MANTHAN 3.0

CHATURANGA

-Battle of the Brains

Instructions

  1. Each team should consist of 2 members.
  2. Any number of teams can participate.
  3. The first round will be a written test of multiple-choice questions (MCQ) type, where each team will be given a set of 25 general knowledge questions
  4. Top 10 teams will be qualified for the next round The round will have question based on visuals and audio.
  5. Top 5 teams from the previous round will get qualified to the last round. This round will have questions related to Tech.
  6. Candidates are Strictly prohibited from communicating with other teams. such teams will be disqualified from the competition.
  7. If there is a Tie between the teams then there will be a Tie-Breaker round.
  8. Judges decision will be considered as final.

Scoring Criteria

  1. In First round each question carries one mark.
  2. In First round each question carries one mark.
  3. In Final round each question carries 20 points and there will be Minus 10 points for each wrong answer.
  4. Winner and RunnerUp will be decided by the total points scored in the Final round.

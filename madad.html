TECH-MANTHAN 3.0

AdFiesta

~ A Festivity of Frenzied Ads

Instructions

  1. Maximum number of participants in a team is 6.
  2. Time limit: 6 minutes.
  3. Multiple teams can participate from each class.
  4. The product should be related to technology.
  5. The presentation should contain brand name, tagline for the selected product.
  6. Participants are allowed to use props or costumes to enhance their ads.
  7. The decision of the judges will be the final and binding.
  8. No exposure to biasness against any caste/creed/community or religion is permitted.
  9. Judges can ask questions about the content or product.

Judging Criteria

  1. Content, creativity, originality.
  2. Humor.
  3. Teamwork.
  4. Clarity and message.
